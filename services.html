e-Patient

Doctor will be able to see medical history of patient by entering his/her ID

Doctor will add his opinions, prescription and next appointment (if needed)

Other lab tests and supporting tests will be recorded in system against each patient by representative


e-Record

Representative of medical store will be able to check before giving a medicine that either the person is asking for prescribed medicine or not

e-Prescription


Patients will be able to see medical history recorded by doctor

Patients will be able to recieve notifications about prescriptions and next appointments

Patients will be able to take medicines with e-prescription




e-Health Record System

e-HealthRecord System designed for Pakistan  - Poznan University of Technology 2018
